Finding a great health plan

Health insurance is tough. Stride Health crunches the numbers for consumers and recommends plans based on their quality and affordability. I led design through the 2014-2015 Open Enrollment period. We designed and launch a complete rebuild of the product experience in four months, transitioning it from a single page desktop app to a fully responsive, mobile first experience.

Partnering with the on-demand economy

I designed and spec-ed integrations with the best companies in the on-demand economy, including Uber, Postmates and TaskRabbit, with the goal of helping their 1099 workers find health insurance.

Responsive framework and design guidelines

In software, great design should be accessible. The design language of the product needs to be available to individual designers, engineers building new features on their own and external partners. I designed and developed a comprehensive set of guidelines that exists in three forms: stylesheets in the app, a PDF document and a Sketch template file.
