About me

Hi, I'm a product designer who lives in the SF Bay Area. I studied Symbolic Systems and Human Computer Interaction at Stanford University and graduated with my BS in 2010. Since then, I've led product and design teams at Reputation.com and Stride Health.

I enjoy topics related to machine learning, public health, nutrition, education and public policy. I like big consumer markets. I hope to help individuals and families who are competing in a world of increasing social inequity and labor automation.
